# Level 1: Conversation — Getting Real Value from Chat
# Level 1：對話——從聊天中獲得真正的價值

> *"The simplest AI interaction is a conversation. But simple doesn't mean shallow — if you know what to ask."*
> *「最簡單的 AI 互動是對話。但簡單不代表膚淺——如果你知道該問什麼。」*

---

## What This Level Covers 這一級涵蓋什麼

You're at Level 1 if you use ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any LLM through a chat interface. This guide helps you get dramatically more value from conversations — before you need any tools, any files, any code.

如果你使用 ChatGPT、Claude、Gemini 或任何透過聊天介面的 LLM，你就在 Level 1。這份指南幫助你從對話中獲得顯著更多的價值——在你需要任何工具、檔案或程式碼之前。

---

## The Five Conversation Patterns That Actually Work 五個真正有效的對話模式

### Pattern 1: Give Context Before Questions 在提問前給脈絡

❌ "Write me a project update email."
✅ "I'm a project manager for a government cultural affairs project. My client is the city's Cultural Bureau. They prefer formal but warm tone, not bureaucratic. Write a project update email covering: 3 events completed, 2 upcoming, budget at 65% utilization."

The difference: a generic answer vs. an answer that sounds like **you** wrote it.

差別：通用答案 vs. 聽起來像**你**寫的答案。

### Pattern 2: Assign a Role 指定角色

"You are an experienced accountant reviewing this expense report. Check for common errors, miscategorized items, and anything that would flag an audit."

「你是一位經驗豐富的會計師，正在審查這份報帳表。檢查常見錯誤、歸類錯誤的項目，以及任何會引起審計注意的事項。」

This activates the LLM's domain-specific knowledge patterns. Same brain, different lens.

這啟動了 LLM 的領域特定知識模式。同一個大腦，不同的鏡片。

### Pattern 3: Show the Format You Want 展示你要的格式

"Summarize this in the following format:
**Executive Summary**: (3 sentences max)
**Key Numbers**: (bullet points)
**Risks**: (table with columns: Risk, Likelihood, Impact, Mitigation)
**Recommendation**: (1 paragraph)"

Don't hope the AI guesses your format. Show it.

不要期望 AI 猜中你的格式。展示它。

### Pattern 4: Iterate, Don't Restart 迭代，不要重來

Your first prompt rarely produces the final result. The real skill is **refinement**:

你的第一個提示很少產出最終結果。真正的技能是**精煉**：

- "Good, but make the tone more formal" 「好，但語調要更正式」
- "Keep the structure, but the budget section needs more detail" 「保持結構，但預算部分需要更多細節」
- "This is too long. Cut it to half the length but keep all the numbers" 「太長了。縮到一半長度但保留所有數字」

Each refinement adds your judgment. After 3-4 rounds, the output reflects your standards.

每次精煉加入你的判斷。經過 3-4 輪，輸出反映你的標準。

### Pattern 5: Ask It to Check Itself 要求它自我檢查

"Now review what you just wrote. Are there any claims without evidence? Any inconsistencies? Anything that doesn't match what I told you earlier?"

「現在審查你剛寫的。有沒有沒有證據的聲稱？有不一致的地方嗎？有沒有不符合我之前告訴你的？」

LLMs can catch their own mistakes when explicitly asked to look for them.

LLM 被明確要求時可以抓到自己的錯誤。

## Exercises 練習

### Exercise 1: The Better Prompt 更好的提示

Take something you recently asked an AI to do. Rewrite the prompt using Patterns 1-3 above. Compare the outputs.

拿一件你最近要 AI 做的事。用上面的模式 1-3 重寫提示。比較輸出。

### Exercise 2: The Refinement Chain 精煉鏈

Start with a simple request. Refine it at least 4 times, each time adding a specific improvement. Notice how the 4th version is dramatically better than the 1st — and that **your judgment** is what made the difference.

從一個簡單的請求開始。精煉至少 4 次，每次加入一個特定的改善。注意第 4 版比第 1 版好多少——而**你的判斷**是造成差異的原因。

### Exercise 3: The Self-Review 自我審查

Ask the AI to produce something. Then ask it to review its own work with specific criteria you provide. Notice what it catches and what it misses. The things it misses? Those are your **domain expertise** — and they're exactly what Level 3 Skills will capture.

要 AI 產出某些東西。然後要它用你提供的特定標準審查自己的工作。注意它抓到什麼、漏掉什麼。它漏掉的？那些就是你的**領域專業知識**——也正是 Level 3 Skills 會捕捉的。

## When You've Outgrown Level 1 當你超越 Level 1 的時候

You'll know it's time for Level 2 when:

- You keep copying text from files and pasting it into the chat 你一直從檔案複製文字貼到對話裡
- You wish it could "just look at" your spreadsheet 你希望它能「直接看」你的試算表
- You want the output saved as a real file, not just chat text 你想要輸出存為真正的檔案，不只是聊天文字
- You're re-explaining the same context every session 你每次工作階段都在重新解釋同樣的背景

These are signals that you need **tools** — the subject of [Level 2](level-2-tools.md).

這些是你需要**工具**的訊號——[Level 2](level-2-tools.md) 的主題。
